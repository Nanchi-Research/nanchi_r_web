Our strategic plan for the first half of 2025 - goals, milestones and future directions

2025 First Half Roadmap

Q1 Mar-Apr 2025

Synthetic Data Generation Model

Development of a GAN-based synthetic data generation framework for associative models. This will enable researchers to generate high-quality synthetic datasets when real data is scarce or sensitive.

AI GAN Data Generation
Q2 Apr-Jun 2025

Mathematics Cheat Sheet

Create comprehensive math reference materials for beginners covering the basic concepts.

Math Education Resources
Q2 Apr 2025

Website Development

Launch of the official Nanchi Research website to serve as the central hub for all projects, documentation, and community interactions.

Website Platform Community
Q2 Apr-May 2025

Quantum Machine Learning

Exploration of hybrid quantum-classical models to accelerate machine learning tasks, particularly in optimization and kernel methods.

Quantum ML Research

Community Growth

Initiatives to attract new participants to the Nanchi research community.

Community Growth Outreach

Dynamic system development

  • Animation of the system evolution
  • State stability analysis
